Paris, France


Paris, the capital of France, is a global center for art, fashion, and culture. Known as the "City of Light," Paris offers an unmatched blend of historical and modern attractions, making it one of the most popular tourist destinations in the world.

  1. Eiffel Tower - The iconic symbol of Paris.
  2. Louvre Museum - The world's largest art museum, home to the Mona Lisa.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral - A Gothic masterpiece.
  4. Champs-Élysées - The most famous avenue for shopping and dining.
  5. Montmartre - A historic district known for its art and cafes.
  • Croissants - The famous buttery pastry.
  • Escargot - Snails cooked in garlic butter, a French delicacy.
  • Crêpes - Thin pancakes filled with sweet or savory ingredients.
  • Baguette - The traditional French bread loaf.
  • Ratatouille - A classic vegetable stew from southern France.
