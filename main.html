Paris, France


Paris is known as the "City of Light" and is one of the most romantic and beautiful cities in the world. It is famous for its art, architecture, food, and culture.

  1. Eiffel Tower - The iconic symbol of Paris and a must-see landmark.
  2. Louvre Museum - The world's largest art museum, home to the Mona Lisa.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral - A masterpiece of French Gothic architecture.
  4. Champs-Élysées - The most famous avenue in Paris, lined with shops and cafes.
  5. Sacré-Cœur Basilica - A beautiful basilica with a stunning view of the city.
  • Croissants - Flaky, buttery pastries that are a staple of French breakfast.
  • Macarons - Colorful, sweet treats that melt in your mouth.
  • Escargot - A traditional French dish of snails cooked with garlic and butter.
  • Baguette - Freshly baked bread with a crispy crust and soft interior.
  • Crème brûlée - A classic French dessert with a creamy custard base and caramelized top.
